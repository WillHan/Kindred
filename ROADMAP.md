# Kindred — Roadmap

*Living plan. Simple on purpose. We test one thing at a time and change fast when something doesn't work.*

## The one belief we're testing
The hard part of AI job loss is a slide people go through alone: **stress → anxiety → depression.** If we meet people **early** — with content that makes them feel seen, and a safe place to land — we can help them prepare before it deepens.

## The three parts (one movement)
1. **Content (the front door)** — short videos + music that name the feeling. People feel seen first. *(This already exists and already reaches people.)*
2. **Kindred (the place to land)** — an opt-in, AI-assisted, human-in-the-loop space that listens and points to real help. Never therapy, never diagnosis. See [SAFETY.md](./SAFETY.md).
3. **Non-profit (what makes it last)** — AI-first keeps support cheap; non-profit means we serve the people no company can make money from.

## Now — the fail-fast test (2 weeks, ~$0)
**Question:** Can caring, AI-made content reach people who have gone quiet — and get a few to take one small step?

- Make 6–8 short feeling-first videos (building on the album + the video that already reached ~1,400 people).
- End each with one soft line: *"If this is you, there's a quiet place here."* → a simple link. No login. No judgment.
- Run for 2 weeks.

**Pass:** a real handful of quiet viewers click, and ~10+ people land on the page or leave one private note.
**Fail / change:** lots of views, almost no clicks → "reaching the quiet," as built, doesn't work yet. We change the approach before spending more.

## Next (only if the test passes)
- Bring in a **mental-health advisor** and a **person with lived experience**.
- Stand up the **landing space** with the safety workflow (crisis routing to 9-8-8, human-in-the-loop).
- Set up a **fiscal sponsor** so we can accept grants and pay a small stipend — only when a grant needs it.
- Apply for the funding that fits an early, validated pilot (e.g. Emergent Ventures).

## Later (only with real evidence)
- A measurable pilot that shows we reach or support quiet people **safely** — no surveillance, no auto-outreach, no unsafe crisis automation.
- That evidence is the key that unlocks bigger support (foundations, government, AI-lab programs).

## How we'll know it's working
- Quiet people taking **one small step** (a click, an opt-in, a private note) — not likes or follower counts.
- People telling us they felt **seen** and found a **real next step**.

## Kill / pause rules
We stop or change any path that needs: diagnosis, therapy, risk-scoring, or auto-outreach to individuals before we have proper safety governance; or that runs more than ~60–90 days with no income and no partner traction. (Full list in [SAFETY.md](./SAFETY.md).)
